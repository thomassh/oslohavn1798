About us

The Port of Oslo in 1798 has been made by Tidvis AS. Starting as a research project studying 18th century Norwegian trade, we have developed into a firm that aims at disseminating history in new ways for a wider audience.

The historians in this project are Ragnhild Hutchison (project manager), Amund Pedersen and Henrik Thommesen. Designers are Mats Jørgensen, Anders Hereng Hansen (3D) and Thomas Sørlie Hansen (design, illustration and web).
